How to Join AllegiantVETS

We’re excited you’re interested in joining our community of veterans, mentors, and mission-driven professionals. The process to get started is simple and designed to meet you where you are in your transition.

What members get
1:1 Mentorship Weekly Accountability Study Cohorts Resume Reviews Partner Intros & Discounts Job Search Support
  1. 1. Apply Online: Complete our brief application form so we can get to know you and your goals.
  2. 2. Join Discord: Once accepted, you'll be invited to our Discord server where most communication and community accountability takes place.
  3. 3. Attend Orientation: We'll walk you through how to succeed in the program, complete weekly surveys, and connect with a mentor.
  4. 4. Set Your Goals: Choose your focus (e.g., cybersecurity, cloud, IT support), and begin working toward your certifications and job-readiness.
