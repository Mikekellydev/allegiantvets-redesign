How to Join AllegiantVETS

Step 1: Fill Out the Interest Form

Start by completing our onboarding form so we can learn more about your goals and background.

Complete the Form

Step 2: Join the Discord Community

Our community lives on Discord — connect with mentors, staff, and other veterans navigating the same journey.

Join the Discord

Step 3: Get Matched with a Mentor

After joining, you'll be introduced to mentors based on your goals and skills. They’ll guide you throughout your SkillBridge journey.

Step 4: Begin Training and Tracking

Gain access to certification pathways, weekly check-ins, and valuable resources to help you launch a civilian tech career.
