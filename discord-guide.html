Discord User Guide

Follow these quick steps to join our community and get connected with mentors and peers.

  1. Create a Discord account (free).
  2. Join our server with your invite link.
  3. Introduce yourself in the welcome channel.
  4. Pick your learning track and join the cohort channel.
  5. Turn on notifications for important updates.
