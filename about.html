About AllegiantVETS

AllegiantVETS is a veteran-led nonprofit committed to helping transitioning service members and veterans discover fulfilling careers in tech, cybersecurity, and IT through mentorship, community accountability, and industry-recognized certification programs.

Our mission is to walk alongside each veteran, empowering them with real-world skills, professional development, and the support they need to thrive during and after military transition.
