Our Mission

AllegiantVETS is a nonprofit dedicated to helping veterans and transitioning service members bridge the gap to the civilian workforce through mentorship, certification, and a vibrant community.

Our Story

Founded by veterans, for veterans, AllegiantVETS began with the desire to create real, actionable paths for those who served. We've grown into a nationwide community with impact in IT, cloud, and cybersecurity careers.

Meet Our Team

Our leadership team includes former military professionals, career advisors, and IT experts who understand the unique journey of military transition. They are committed to seeing you succeed beyond the uniform.
