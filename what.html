What We Do

AllegiantVETS provides a holistic, personalized, and mission-ready approach to military transition. From SkillBridge placements to mentorship and upskilling, our team ensures that each service member receives the resources and support they need to thrive in the civilian workforce.

Guided Career Transition

Whether you're weeks away from separation or just beginning to plan, our process supports you from first connection to final employment:

  • 1-on-1 coaching and weekly accountability
  • Customized SkillBridge pathways
  • VA benefit education and career mapping
  • Access to exclusive partners and career tools

SkillBridge Success Support

As a DoD-approved SkillBridge provider, AllegiantVETS connects active duty members with training and real-world job experience before they separate. We guide you through every step of the application, placement, and transition.

Opportunity Creation

Through strategic industry partnerships, we open doors to high-impact, high-opportunity careers in fields such as IT, government contracting, logistics, and professional services. Our network helps you go from MOS to meaningful career with intention.

Support Beyond Separation

Our community doesn’t stop after you get the job. We offer long-term connection, mentorship, and alumni support so you can continue building your legacy with other mission-minded professionals.
