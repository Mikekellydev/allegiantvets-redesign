What We Do

AllegiantVETS exists to empower veterans, transitioning service members, and military spouses to succeed in the civilian workforce — especially in IT, cybersecurity, and cloud computing.

Our Mission

We serve as a bridge between military experience and thriving tech careers by offering mentorship, accountability, and training support in a unified community.

How We Help

  • 🏁 **Onboarding Support** — Learn how to navigate SkillBridge, job searching, and professional networking.
  • 🎯 **Weekly Accountability** — Stay on track with goal setting, progress reporting, and partner check-ins.
  • 🎓 **Training + Certifications** — Access top-tier IT certification courses through our Coursera and partner platforms.
  • 🤝 **Mentorship & Peer Support** — Get advice from veterans who have successfully transitioned into civilian careers.
  • 📁 **Job Search Help** — Improve your resume, LinkedIn, and interview skills with feedback and job leads.
  • 🌐 **Community Access** — Join our Discord and live sessions to connect, collaborate, and grow.
