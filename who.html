AllegiantVETS

Our Mission

AllegiantVETS is committed to investing in today’s heroes by providing a strategic approach to career transition, empowering military service members and their families to successfully enter the civilian workforce.

Our Values

  • Accountability and transparency
  • Mission-first mindset with people-centered support
  • Integrity, honor, and commitment
  • Diversity and inclusion across communities and industries

Our Impact

We’ve supported thousands of veterans through over 6,500 hours of personalized transition guidance and empowered career success with a 98% completion rate for our SkillBridge candidates.

Meet Our Founder

Marcus Haney, a US Army veteran, founded AllegiantVETS to solve the challenges he saw during his own transition. His leadership and passion have guided AllegiantVETS to national recognition and measurable impact.

Diversity & Inclusion

AllegiantVETS celebrates diversity and works to create inclusive pathways for all veterans, spouses, and underserved populations, supporting equitable opportunities in technology, government, and private industry.
