Frequently Asked Questions

What is AllegiantVETS?
AllegiantVETS is a veteran-led nonprofit helping transitioning service members gain career guidance, certifications, and community support for IT and cybersecurity jobs.
How do I join the program?
Click “Join Now” on the homepage or visit the How to Join page. You’ll be directed to apply and join our Discord community.
What are accountability surveys?
Weekly surveys help you stay on track with goals, hours, partner check-ins, and progress. It’s part of what keeps our community strong.
Is this just for veterans?
AllegiantVETS is primarily for transitioning service members and veterans, but civilian allies and mentors are also welcome to support and participate.
