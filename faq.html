Have Questions? We’ve Got Answers.

Explore answers to the most common questions about SkillBridge, OJT, Discord access, and more.

What is AllegiantVETS?

We’re a community-driven support network helping transitioning service members succeed in the civilian workforce, especially in IT and tech.

How do I join?

Click “Join Now” on the homepage or visit the How to Join page for step-by-step instructions to get onboarded and into the Discord community.

Is this program official SkillBridge?

No, AllegiantVETS is a community support resource. However, we help you stay accountable, connected, and goal-driven while completing your approved SkillBridge with other organizations.

What’s required in the weekly survey?

Your code word, hours, goals, partner connection, and optional comments. It helps you stay engaged and your mentor team stay informed.
