Frequently Asked Questions

What is AllegiantVETS?

AllegiantVETS is a veteran-led organization helping transitioning service members succeed in IT and tech careers through mentorship, certification support, and community empowerment.

How does SkillBridge work with AllegiantVETS?

SkillBridge allows active-duty service members to intern with companies during their last 180 days of service. AllegiantVETS partners with employers to place you in valuable IT-focused roles.

Do I need IT experience to join?

No prior experience is required. Many of our members are starting from scratch. We provide the roadmap and support to help you grow into tech roles.

How do I track my weekly progress?

Each week, you’ll complete an accountability survey to log your activities, hours, and goals. This helps you stay on track and allows us to support you better.

Where can I connect with other members?

Join our Discord server and attend weekly community calls, mentorship check-ins, and Empowerment Week events to build strong connections.
