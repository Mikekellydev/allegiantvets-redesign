Join AllegiantVETS

AllegiantVETS is a nonprofit helping transitioning service members, veterans, and military spouses launch successful careers in IT, cybersecurity, and technology.

Through career mentorship, weekly accountability, certification planning, and a nationwide network of supporters, we walk alongside you — from the moment you start transition to your first civilian job and beyond.

Who Can Join?

  • Active-duty service members in SkillBridge or transition
  • Veterans and reservists
  • Military spouses and civilian allies committed to support

What You’ll Get

  • Mentorship from veterans in tech
  • Career certification planning and job-hunting support
  • A supportive, accountability-driven community
  • Access to Discord and weekly check-ins
