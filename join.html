Join AllegiantVETS

Getting started with AllegiantVETS is easy. Whether you're a transitioning service member, veteran, or military spouse, we have the resources and support ready for you.

Steps to Get Started

  1. Apply to SkillBridge: Complete the intake form to begin your onboarding process.
  2. Join Our Discord: After applying, you’ll receive an invite to our private Discord server where you can connect with staff, mentors, and peers.
  3. Attend Orientation: Learn how the program works and get set up for success.
  4. Submit Your Weekly Surveys: Stay accountable and track your progress toward transition goals.
